# SimmerDown: Budget-Friendly Healthy Recipes for Students, Young Adults, and Families

SimmerDown is a custom WordPress theme designed to make healthy eating accessible and practical. The site provides simple, affordable, and nutritious meal ideas for people with limited time, ingredients, or financial resources.

Users can:
- Browse recipes filtered by dietary needs, preparation time, and budget
- Search recipes based on ingredients they already have at home
- Submit their own recipes and reviews
- Save favorite recipes and create collections

Website Goals

Target Audience
- University students looking for quick, affordable meals
- Young adults learning to cook on a budget
- Families seeking healthy, cost-effective recipes
- Anyone interested in reducing food waste by using available ingredients

Purpose
1. Make healthy eating accessible by challenging the myth that healthy food is expensive or complicated
2. Reduce food waste by helping users cook with ingredients they already have
3. Build community through user-submitted recipes, reviews, and ratings
4. Educate users on budget-friendly cooking techniques and ingredient substitutions
